Independent - Shambler/Убойник (On pause)

As a tribute to Artyom in the Metro books by Dmitry Glukhovsky, I decided to model his most untrusted weapon, Убойник. So aptly named murderer when translated. A double action, revolving shell loaded shotgun made with scrap AK components.



*On pause until I am done with undergraduate university*

Objectives:

  • CAD the Shambler based on photos and in-game footage
  • Fabricate the model so that can be operated as a gas operated airsoft replica

Challenges:

  • Being a 'puppy-loving pacifist', I had to learn about gun mechanisms before the model could be made functional
  • The significantly complex project forced me to create several test rigs focused on specific mechanisms

Results (So FAR):

  • A first draft CAD model mostly matching the images in game
  • A second draft with test rigs fabricated to add functionality
  • The main test rig is the shotgun bolt loading and barrel rotating mechanism that is already 3D printed
