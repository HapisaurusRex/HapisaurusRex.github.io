Academic - Overseer

For my intro to engineering class, my team chose to program an arduino to address Sudden Infant Death Syndrome (SIDS). This was done with a total cost below $100

Summary

Objectives:

  • To monitor infant vital signs (breathing and heart rate)
  • To alert medical personnel of the specific medical issue (error with breathing, heart rate or both)

Challenges:

  • This was my first engineering focused project and I had limited experience with an Arduino and had to learn quick.
  • To my knowledge, there is no known breathing rate sensor so I had to make my own

Results:

  • A device that can generate different sound and visual cues to quickly allow medical personnel to diagnose an issue with the infant monitored.
  • A variable resistance wire wrapped around the chest that could measure infant breathing rate without being invasive.
